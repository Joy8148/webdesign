Anbanathapuram Vahaira Charities College of Arts and Sciences is an autonomous college located in Mayiladuthurai, India. It was founded in 1955, moved to its present site in 1957, and received autonomous status in 1987.

Antelope Valley College is a good community college because of the quality to cost ratio. If you go to a 4-year you will absolutely have a better experience but if you apply yourself, you can learn just as well here compared to a 4 year.

AVC College of Engineering has maintained good ranks over the years and is ranked at #101-150 in the 'Colleges' category by the NIRF ranking 2024.
